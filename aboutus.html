About the SYOK

Everything Syok-ing Happening in the World? You can bet Syok News will report on it. From strange sightings to unexplainable phenomena, Syok News will have you covered. Syok News is mostly known for their sighting of the Flying Frying Pan monster and the Moon being Up in the Sky at 1PM.


Established in 2022 by Low Yi Ting, the idea of this website came up when she first seen an object in the sky in broad daylight. Turns out it was the Moon itself being visible at a time where the Sun dominates the daylight sky.
